All About Me!

Bio

Born in Mukilteo, and educated in Seattle, Sean is passionate about running, reading, drinking coffee, making friends, and coding. He enjoys music, being in nature, and being creative. Sean has lived his entire life in the northwest, and he plans to continue doing so.

Education

Sean attended high school in Mukilteo, went to the University of Washington, majoring in English and graduating Phi Beta Kappa, completed a Master's in Teaching with Western Washington University, and is now exploring a development career with Code Fellows.

Jobs

Over the years, Sean has had a number of jobs, including:

  • working as an usher, greeting people,
  • a barista, serving up coffee,
  • a student teacher, educating high-schoolers,
  • a para educator, serving special education students.

Goals

Some goals of Sean's include becoming proficient in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, along with the hope of one day becoming a front-end developer.
